Yuanlu Xu

Research Scientist

Meta Superintelligence Labs, Meta Platforms, Inc.

I am a Research Scientist at Meta Superintelligence Labs. My research focuses on 3D vision and graphics, digital avatars, computer vision and machine learning, generative models, and vision-language models. I have published extensively at top-tier venues including CVPR, ICCV, ECCV, NeurIPS, AAAI, and IEEE TPAMI, with over 3,100 citations and an h-index of 22.

Research Interests

3D Vision & Graphics

3D scene understanding, reconstruction, and rendering from images and videos.

Digital Avatars

Animatable reconstruction of clothed humans and realistic digital human creation.

CV / ML

Computer vision and machine learning for pose estimation, tracking, and recognition.

Generative Models

Diffusion-based and GAN-based methods for 3D content generation and editing.

VLM / MLLM

Vision-language models and multimodal large language models for visual understanding.
